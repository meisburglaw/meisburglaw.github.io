Who I Help

At the practice of John Marshall Meisburg, Jr., Esquire, we are dedicated to providing expert legal services across a broad range of practice areas. With over 50 years of legal experience, Mr. Meisburg offers comprehensive assistance to clients facing diverse and challenging legal issues, including:

  • Private Judge/Mediation/Arbitration: As a skilled mediator and arbitrator, Mr. Meisburg facilitates effective resolutions outside of traditional court proceedings. His extensive experience as a former U.S. Administrative Law Judge equips him with the expertise to guide parties through private adjudication and mediation processes with impartiality and fairness.
  • Employment Law/Federal Personnel Law: We assist employees and employers in navigating complex employment law issues, including federal personnel matters. Whether you are facing disputes related to workplace rights, employment contracts, or federal employment regulations, Mr. Meisburg provides knowledgeable and strategic legal counsel.
  • Social Security Disability/VA Disability: For those seeking Social Security Disability or Veterans Affairs (VA) benefits, Mr. Meisburg offers compassionate and thorough representation. He helps clients understand their rights and advocates for the benefits they deserve, leveraging his deep understanding of the application and appeals processes.
  • Sexual Harassment: Mr. Meisburg is committed to supporting victims of sexual harassment by providing a safe and effective avenue for seeking justice. He works to ensure that individuals are heard and that their claims are addressed with the utmost seriousness and professionalism. Notably, he filed and litigated the landmark U.S. Supreme Court case on sexual harassment (Mechelle Vinson v. Meritor Savings Bank).
  • Whistleblower Reprisal: If you have experienced retaliation for exposing wrongdoing, Mr. Meisburg is here to help. He offers robust legal support for whistleblowers facing reprisals, working to protect their rights and hold accountable those responsible for retaliation. He notably filed and litigated the first whistleblower reprisal case with the U.S. Office of Special Counsel (MSPB) (OSC vs. Jerome Hoban).
  • Religious Freedom/Civil Rights: Protecting your constitutional rights is a priority at our practice. Mr. Meisburg is dedicated to defending religious freedom and civil rights, advocating for individuals facing discrimination or violations of their fundamental freedoms.
  • Family Law/Mediation: In matters of family law, including divorce, child custody, and support issues, Mr. Meisburg provides sensitive and skilled representation. His mediation services aim to foster amicable solutions and ensure fair outcomes for all parties involved.
  • Criminal Law: For those navigating the complexities of criminal charges, Mr. Meisburg offers experienced defense counsel. He is committed to safeguarding your rights and providing vigorous representation in all criminal matters.

Whatever your legal needs, John Marshall Meisburg, Jr. is here to offer the dedicated support and expert guidance you require. Contact us today to discuss how we can assist you.
